Gandalf

Battles fearsom beasts and is often seen with hobbits and dwarves.

About

Gandalf the Grey is a fictional character created by J.R.R. Tolkien in the childrens book "The Hobbit" in 1937. He is one of the five wizards in middle earth and among the greater of the five, second only to Saruman until Gandalf defeated him and took his place as the white wizard, becoming Gandalf the White.

Gandalf has appeared both in the Hobbit seriese as well as the Lord of the Rings seriese.

Companions

As previousely stated Gandalf is one of the five wizards in middle earth, however he does also have many friends throughout the lands.

Each of these characters has been shown to have a friendly, student teacher type of relationship with him.
